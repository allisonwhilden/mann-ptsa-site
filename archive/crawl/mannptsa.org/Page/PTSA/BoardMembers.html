Co-Presidents

president@mannptsa.org

Bruno

Amy

  • Conducts Board and General Membership Meetings
  • Makes appointments to Nomination, Financial Review, and Awards committees
  • Attends PTSA council meetings and reports on info about WSPTA and LWPTSA programs
  • Communicates regularly with the school principal
  • Works with Co-VP's of Events & Fundraising and school administration to set PTSA event calendar for the school year
  • Completes the Standards of Affiliation Agreement
  • Serves as an ex-officio member of all committees except Nominating and Financial Committees

Co-Treasurers

treasurer@mannptsa.org

David

Becca

  • Leads financial operations of the PTSA
  • Keeps a record and issues all funds received and distributed through the PTSA
  • Prepares a monthly financial report for the board of directors
  • Serves as chair of the budget committee and presents the budget to the members

Co-VP's of Advocacy / F.A.C.E. (Family and Community Engagement)

advocacy@mannptsa.org

Morgan

OPEN
  • Leads efforts to support students, families, and the broader school community through advocacy and engagement initiatives with a focus on inclusivity across gender, race, LGBTQ+, abilities and other identities. This role supports the following initiatives and oversees their corresponding committees:
  • Emergency Preparedness – Ensures the PTSA supports school safety and readiness.
  • Legislative – Liaises with the district PTSA council and supports their local/state advocacy efforts.
  • Sustainability – Promotes environmental efforts within the school community.
  • Colt Community Caring – Supports underserved students through scholarships and resources.
  • Special Needs – Advocates for and supports students with special needs and their families within the Horace Mann community. This position works to ensure that all students have access to the resources and support they need to thrive academically and socially.
  • Quest – Supports the needs of highly capable students.

Time Commitment: 5-10 hours per month, including meetings and coordination with the board and chair leads.

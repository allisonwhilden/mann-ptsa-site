Math is Cool Championship: Registration Now Open for Grades 4 and 5!

Math Is Cool competition offers team-based problem solving and individual challenges to make learning fun, collaborative, and inspiring. 
Event Details
  • Grade 4 Competition
    • Location: Lincoln High School, Seattle
    • Date: March 21, 2026
    • Max: 5 teams of 4 students each
  • Grade 5 Competition
    • Location: Liberty High School, Renton
    • Date: March 7, 2026
    • Time: 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM
    • Max: 5 teams of 4 students each
Selection Process and Deadline to register: December 7th, 2025

Due to space limitations, only 20 students per grade will be selected to participate. If interest exceeds this number, we will host an internal individual exam to determine eligibility.

  • Date of Internal Exam: January 20th, 2026, from 4 to 4:45 pm
  • Format: 35 minutes, 40 questions (similar to the Math Is Cool individual test)
  • Exam Date: To be finalized after collecting interest (Tentative: January)
  • Spots are limited! If your child is interested, please complete the registration form by the deadline
Registration Form: Math is Cool Championship 2025 - 26
Participation Fee
 A $20 fee per student will be collected once the final list of participants is confirmed.

Biweekly Math Challenges (Free for All!)

Open to all K-5 students • No sign-up required • Fun prizes!

Join our biweekly problem-solving activities from September through May.
  • Monthly Prize Drawings: One winner per grade level will be randomly selected from all entries that meet the minimum number of correct answers.
  • End-of-Year Trophies: Awarded for most challenges submitted and top scorers.

How to Participate:

  1. Get the challenge: Pick up from office, print from mathinaction.org, or solve digitally.
  2. Solve problems: Work on them with family and friends, share strategies, and try as many as you can (going beyond the minimum is encouraged!).
  3. Submit by Thursday 11:59pm: Turn in answers online via the form in the table below. Please submit a separate form for each child. Note that sometimes you need to either answer or select "want to skip" the last question for further questions to show up.

  4. Challenge release and submission deadlines: For specific dates, please refer to the calendar below to stay on track and plan ahead.

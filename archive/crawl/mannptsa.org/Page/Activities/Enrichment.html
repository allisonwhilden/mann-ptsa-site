ENRICHMENT & MUSIC CLASSES

We are excited to offer a diverse range of Courses to enrichment your child's learning experience beyond the classroom.  From sports to arts to music to STEM, there's something for every child to enjoy and learn from!

MUSIC Class OPTIONS

Horace Mann PTSA is please to help bring choir, orchestra, and band class options to our enrichment offerings.  Unlike other enrichment classes, music classes will be for the full school year.  Enrollment will be open the same time as Fall enrichment classes.  Students are welcome to join choir at any time.  Enrollment for choir will stay open all year.  

Instruments: students are responsible to obtain their own instruments as well as the beginner music book. (details coming soon)  Music and Arts in Redmond offer affordable rentals.  If the cost of renting an instrument is a barrier for any student, please reach out and PTSA volunteers will help obtain a free instrument that your student may borrow.

We encourage all interested families to read more about Band and Orchestra on here and/or attend the Information Night on September 24, 6:30-7:30 pm.

Additional Details

Financial Support

We offer financial aid for any families that need it.  No questions asked.  Please reach out to  Ms. Ginn cginn@lwsd.org or enrichment@mannptsa.org and they will provide you with a code to enter at check out for $100 off tuition.  Families may have 1 code per child per year.  Families with financial need will also be prioritized when selecting chaperones for class.  The child of the parent chaperons is enrolled in the class for free.  Apply HERE

Duration

Courses run for 6 to 12 weeks, depending on the activity.

Timing

Classes typically take place directly after school 4-5pm Mon, Tue, Thu and 2:30-3:30 PM Wed. There will be limited classes available before school from 8:10-9:10am

Locations

Activities are held right at Horace Mann in various school facilities, including the gym, art & science room, and music room.

 Enrichment Chaperones - FREE Tuition

Get involved as a chaperone! As a bonus for chaperoning your child's class, they get free tuition and a guaranteed spot in the class.  Apply to chaperone a class HERE.  Chaperones will be selected on the day before enrollment opens.  All applications received by that date will be considered equally.  Once enrollment is open, chaperon positions are assigned on a first come basis.  A class without a dedicated chaperone may be cancelled.  Email enrichment@mannptsa.org if you have questions.  

 

Parent Run Clubs and Activities

We are looking for parent volunteers to run clubs and activities.  If you have an idea of a class or club you would like to run, please reach out to our enrichment coordinator Cindy Goulding enrichment@mannptsa.org.  

 

Note on Tuition and How it's Used

Many of the clubs and enrichment classes are run by the PTSA and not an outside vendor.  The PTSA handles the tuition and material fees collected for these classes and those are generally used for the needs of that class.  However, after a volunteer run club finishes, if there is excess money not used on the club, it will be added to the PTSA general fund.  Excess money from choir will be available for use on the Horace Mann music program through the end of the school year and then also added to the general PTSA fund.

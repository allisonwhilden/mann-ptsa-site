Step Right Up, Colts!

Colt Family Fun Night

Friday, June 12 • 6:00–8:00pm

Horace Mann Parking Lot & Commons

Join us for our final community event of the year! Family Fun Night is a carnival-style evening packed with games, an inflatable obstacle course, face painting, custom balloons, and food trucks. Bring the whole family for an unforgettable night together at Mann.

Wristbands — $5 All-Inclusive

One wristband, one price. Each $5 wristband includes:

🎟️ Game play (carnival games, yard games & prizes)
🎪 Inflatable obstacle course
🎨 Face painting
🎈 Custom balloon twisting

Wristband sales help offset event costs so we can keep this a low-cost, all-inclusive evening for families.

Wristbands available for anyone who needs one — no questions asked. Reach out to Mrs. Ginn ahead of time or ask at the ticket booth the night of the event.

Pre-Sale Tickets

Skip the line! Pre-sale wristbands are available now through Monday, June 8. Pre-sale wristbands will be sent home via teacher mailboxes before the event.

Wristbands can also be purchased the night of the event at the ticket booth — expect to wait in line.

Buy Pre-Sale Wristbands

Pre-sale purchases require a free mannptsa.org account — create yours here.

Inflatable Course Waiver

A signed waiver is required for anyone planning to enter the inflatable obstacle course, and a caregiver must be present. Participants who have signed the waiver receive a yellow wristband at the booth so volunteers can verify at the course entrance.

Pre-sale wristband purchases will include the waiver as part of the checkout process. Anyone purchasing wristbands the night of the event can sign at the ticket booth.

Food Trucks

We’re welcoming local food vendors so families can grab dinner and treats right on site. Vendors take payment directly.

Dandy Dogs

Hot dog meal deal — hot dog, chips & drink for $12.

All City Ice Cream

Scooped and novelty ice cream from the truck.

Gourmini’s

Gourmet mini sandwiches and bites — vegetarian-friendly.

Bhukkad

100% vegetarian and vegan options.

Important to Know

⚠️ This is NOT a drop-off event

Caregiver supervision is required at all times.

🅿️ The parking lot loop closes around 4:30pm after school pick-up — please park on the street
🧘 A calming space will be available in the commons hallway for anyone who needs a break
💵 Vendors and the ticket booth accept card and cash

Volunteers Needed

We really need volunteers to make this event happen! Even a 30-minute slot is a huge help — cover one shift and still enjoy the rest of the night with your family.
